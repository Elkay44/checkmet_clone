About CheckMet

Pioneering the future of attendance management

Our Story

CheckMet was founded in 2019 with a simple vision: to transform the way organizations manage attendance by leveraging cutting-edge facial recognition technology.

Our founders—former HR executives and technology innovators—experienced firsthand the inefficiencies of traditional attendance systems. Time cards were easily manipulated, badges were frequently lost, and fingerprint scanners were often unreliable. These issues resulted in significant administrative overhead and inaccurate time records.

With advancements in facial recognition technology, our team saw an opportunity to create something better: a seamless, accurate, and secure attendance system that would eliminate these pain points for organizations of all sizes.

After two years of intensive research and development, we launched CheckMet's flagship facial recognition attendance solution in 2021. Since then, we've grown to serve over 500 organizations globally, from small businesses to multinational corporations, helping them transform their attendance management processes and gain valuable insights into their workforce.

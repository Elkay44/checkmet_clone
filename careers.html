Why CheckMet?

At CheckMet, we're revolutionizing how organizations manage attendance through innovative facial recognition technology. Our mission is to simplify workforce management while prioritizing security, privacy, and exceptional user experiences.

Our Interview Process

What to expect when applying

1

Application Review

Our hiring team reviews your application and resume to assess your qualifications and experience.

2

Initial Screening

A 30-minute phone or video call with a recruiter to discuss your background, interests, and answer your initial questions.

3

Technical Assessment

For technical roles, a skills-based assessment or coding challenge to evaluate your relevant abilities.

4

Team Interviews

Meet with team members and cross-functional stakeholders to assess both technical and cultural fit.

5

Final Interview

A conversation with a senior leader to discuss your career aspirations and how you align with our mission.

6

Offer & Onboarding

If selected, you'll receive an offer and begin our comprehensive onboarding program.

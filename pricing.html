Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CheckMet pricing and plans

Do I need to purchase hardware separately?

Each of our plans includes a specific number of facial recognition terminals. Standard includes 1 terminal, Professional includes up to 3 terminals, and Enterprise includes unlimited terminals based on your needs. Additional terminals can be purchased separately at the prices listed in the Hardware Options section.

Can I switch plans as my business grows?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time as your business needs change. When upgrading, you'll receive prorated credit for your current plan. Downgrading is available at the end of your billing cycle.

Is there a setup or implementation fee?

There is no additional setup fee for Standard and Professional plans. For Enterprise deployments, implementation services are quoted based on your specific requirements. Hardware installation is included in the price of each terminal.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and government agencies. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and eligibility.

Is there a limit to the number of locations I can manage?

Standard and Professional plans support a single location. Multi-location support is available in the Enterprise plan. Each location requires at least one terminal, which would need to be purchased separately.

Are software updates included in the subscription?

Yes, all plans include regular software updates and security patches at no additional cost. This ensures you always have access to the latest features and security improvements.

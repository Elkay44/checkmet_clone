Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CheckMet's facial recognition system

How secure is the facial recognition?

CheckMet uses enterprise-grade encryption and anti-spoofing AI. All biometric data is encrypted and stored as mathematical templates, not actual images, meeting SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR security standards.

Learn about our security

Can we use fingerprint or card check-in?

Absolutely! While facial recognition is our main focus, CheckMet supports multiple identification methods including fingerprint, smart cards, and PIN codes for complete flexibility.

View all methods

Is CheckMet GDPR compliant?

Yes, privacy and compliance are core to our platform. We've built CheckMet with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations as fundamental requirements, with clear data handling protocols.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will someone respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical support issues, Enterprise customers have access to 24/7 support.

Can I schedule a product demonstration?

Yes! We offer personalized demonstrations of the CheckMet platform. You can request a demo by visiting our demo page or by contacting our sales team directly.

Do you offer on-site consultations?

Yes, for enterprise implementations, we can arrange for on-site consultations and support. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.

How can I get technical support for an existing implementation?

Existing customers can access technical support through our customer portal, by email at support@checkmet.com, or by phone at +1 (800) 555-1235. Our support hours vary by plan level.

I'm interested in becoming a partner. How do I get started?

Visit our Partners page to learn about our partnership program and apply. You can also contact our partnership team directly at partners@checkmet.com.
